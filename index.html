About me

I aquired my B.Sc. and M.Sc. in Computer Engineering at University of Tabriz, Iran. Currently I am a PhD student at Leibniz Universität Hannover in Germany and employed as a research assistant at TIB for the EU-funded Horizon 2020 project CLEOPATRA, under the Marie Skłodowska-Curie Innovative Training Network. My PhD is focused on multimodal analytics of multilingual news articles to infer or differentiate geospatial and temporal information for a given document by leveraging related image content. In general my fields of interests are: deep learning, computer vision, and multimodal analytics of news to name but a few.
